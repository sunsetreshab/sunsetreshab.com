We provide comprehensive residential habilitation services for individuals with developmental and intellectual disabilities in Boise and the Treasure Valley. We are dedicated to helping our clients lead fulfilling and independent lives through personalized care plans and tailored support.

Services

Residential Habilitation

We offer personalized residential habilitation services that are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. Our goal is to promote independence, enhance life skills, and empower our clients to live more independently.

We design tailored programs that promote independence and enhance quality of life. We offer in-home support, assisting clients in living on their own while providing the necessary guidance and care.

Supported Living

We offer supported living programs designed to provide in-home support that aligns with individual needs. Our trusted staff ensures that each client can live in their own home with the assistance they require to function independently. Each personalized program is designed to promote autonomy and self-reliance.

Daily Life Activities

Our skilled staff provide in-home assistance with a variety of daily living activities, including: cooking, cleaning, grocery shopping, and managing money, medications, appointments, and transportation. We provide guidance and support to help clients manage daily tasks, handle finances, coordinate transportation, schedule appointments, and take medications.

Skill Development Programs

We're committed to fostering personal growth and independence. We design programs focused on skill-building and personal growth. Our training programs help individuals develop daily living skills, life skills, and social skills to thrive in their environments. Our personalized programs focus on improving communication, emotional regulation, and social skills.

Behavioral Habilitation

We teach positive coping strategies, stress management techniques, and healthy lifestyle habits. We aim to improve behavior, communication, interaction, and emotional regulation, promoting stronger relationships and a balanced life. Our goal is to empower clients to manage behaviors and thrive on their own.

Independent & Fulfilling Lives
